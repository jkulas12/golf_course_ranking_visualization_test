  • Displayed courses can be filtered by the rankings they appear in. To toggle rankings the ranking rectangle can be clicked. Clicking the "All" or "Public" text will toggle that criteria And clicking "Golf Digest" or "Golf Mag." will do the same.
  • Individual courses can be filtered by their type or by year completed
  • Individual courses can be selected here. Courses can be sorted alphabetically or by a composite rank. Courses can be selected by name by using the "Search Courses" bar. Clicking and dragging the list will highlight a selection of courses on the map. Using the up or down arrow keys will scroll through the list of courses. Additionally, points can be clicked on the map or a group of points can be 'lassoed' by shift+clicking and dragging a selection around a geographical area.
  • All courses for a given architect can be shown by clicking on an architect
